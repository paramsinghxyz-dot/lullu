Privacy Policy

How LuLu Hypermarket collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our app and services.

Last updated: January 2026

Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains how LuLu Group International LLC ("LuLu", "we", "us", or "our") handles your personal data when you download and use the LuLu Hypermarket app. By using our app, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us and information collected automatically when you use our app.

TypeWhat We CollectWhy
Account InfoName, email address, phone number, passwordTo create and manage your account
Order InfoDelivery address, order history, payment method typeTo process and deliver your orders
Device InfoDevice type, OS version, app version, device IDApp functionality and troubleshooting
Usage DataPages viewed, products browsed, search queries, tap eventsTo improve app experience
LocationApproximate location (with permission)To show nearby stores and delivery availability

We do not store full payment card numbers. Payment processing is handled by certified third-party payment processors compliant with PCI-DSS standards.

2

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to operate, improve, and personalise our services:

  • Process and fulfil your orders, including delivery coordination and payment confirmation
  • Send order confirmations, delivery updates, and customer service communications
  • Personalise product recommendations and clearance deal alerts based on your preferences
  • Improve app performance, fix bugs, and develop new features
  • Send promotional offers and sale notifications (you can opt out at any time)
  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents
  • Comply with legal obligations applicable in your country
3

Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only in these limited circumstances:

  • Delivery partners — Your name, address, and phone number are shared with logistics providers to fulfil your order
  • Payment processors — Encrypted payment data is shared with PCI-compliant payment gateways only
  • Analytics providers — Anonymised, aggregated usage data to help us understand app performance
  • Legal requirements — If required by law, court order, or government authority in any country we operate
  • Business transfers — In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of company assets

We never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their own marketing purposes.

4

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on secure servers located within the GCC region. We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • AES-256 encryption for data at rest
  • TLS 1.2+ encryption for all data in transit
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Access controls limiting employee access to personal data on a need-to-know basis
  • Automatic session timeouts and two-factor authentication for staff systems

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active, or as required to provide services and comply with legal obligations. You may request deletion of your account and data at any time by contacting us.

5

Cookies & Tracking

Our app and website use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience. These include:

  • Essential cookies — Required for the app to function (login sessions, cart state)
  • Analytics cookies — Help us understand how users interact with the app (e.g. Google Analytics)
  • Preference cookies — Remember your language, region, and display settings
  • Marketing cookies — Used to show you relevant ads on third-party platforms (optional)

You can manage cookie preferences in your device settings or by contacting us. Disabling essential cookies may affect app functionality.

6

Your Rights

Depending on your country of residence, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction — Ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion — Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Portability — Receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection — Object to processing of your data for marketing purposes
  • Withdrawal of consent — Withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the details in Section 9. We will respond within 30 days.

7

Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately and we will delete it promptly.

Users between 13–18 years of age should use the app only with parental or guardian consent.

8

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or legal requirements. When we make significant changes, we will:

  • Update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page
  • Send a push notification or in-app message informing you of the change
  • In cases of material changes, request fresh consent where required by law

We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the app after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
